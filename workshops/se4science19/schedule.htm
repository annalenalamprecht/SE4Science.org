Workshop Schedule



Session 1

  • Welcome: Introduction of organizers and participants
  • Activity: Ice breaker to identify connections
  • Full paper: Creating Stable Productive CSE Software Development and Integration Processes in Unstable Environments on the Path to Exascale. Roscoe Bartlett and Joseph Frye.
  • Full paper: An HPC perspective on generative programming. Dominic Kempf and Peter Bastian.

Session 2

  • Full paper: Debunking the Myth that Upfront Requirements are Infeasible for Scientific Computing Software. Spencer Smith, Malavika Srinivasan and Sumanth Shankar.
  • Full paper: Challenges for Verifying and Validating Scientific Software in Computational Materials Science. Thomas Vogel, Stephan Druskat, Markus Scheidgen, Claudia Draxl and Lars Grunske.
  • Position paper: Towards Usability as a First-Class Quality of Scientific Software. Reed Milewicz and Paige Rodeghero.
  • Activity: Collection of intermediate insights and thoughts on whiteboard

Lunch break

Session 3 - Caroline Jay (Chair)

  • Full paper: Research Software Development \& Management in Universities: Case Studies from Manchester's RSDS Group, Illinois' NCSA, and Notre Dame's CRC. Daniel S. Katz, Kenton McHenry, Caleb Reinking and Robert Haines.
  • Short paper: Learning units-of-measure from scientific code. Matthew Danish, Miltiadis Allamanis, Marc Brockschmidt, Andrew Rice and Dominic Orchard.
  • Short paper: Releasing Scientific Software in GitHub: A Case Study on SWMM2PEST. Xuanyi Lin, Michelle Simon and Nan Niu.
  • Short paper: Susereum: Towards a Reward Structure for Sustainable Scientific Research Software. Omar Badreddin, Wahab Hamou-Lhadj and Swapnil S Chauhan.

Session 4 - Neil Chue Hong (Chair)

  • Activity: Adding to discussion, challenges and insights on whiteboard
  • Activity: Visiting thoughts and challenges on whiteboard
  • Activity: Moderated group discussion on identified points of interest
  • Activity: Wrap-up with next steps and future direction
